About Us

We are a group of undergraduate students who are creative and passionate about computer science. This project was completed by BU students, Tri Hoang, Vlad Obukhanich, Justin Moy and Ziyu Shen.

Efficient choice

Forget about optical stores. EZ Optic makes the lengthy process from "I need glasses" to "I have glasses" easy. All you have to do is get start with our website.

Innovative Technology

We use machine learning algorithms on our photo dataset to match the glasses type to the facial parameters. With this information, we can check against large data sets in machine learning algorithms and return a pair of glasses that users might wear based on their faces in the online store.
